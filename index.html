    About this tool

    Data sourced from the official Gwinnett County polling locations list (revised October 9, 2025). Coverage includes all 156 Election Day precincts. This is an unofficial community tool — always verify your assignment on the Georgia My Voter Page.

    Problem at the polls? Call the nonpartisan Election Protection hotline: 866-OUR-VOTE (English) · 888-VE-Y-VOTA (Spanish).
